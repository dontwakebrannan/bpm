About

I am an artist currently residing in Portland, Oregon.

My childhood was largely spent somewhat removed from my peers in a small mountain canyon outside of Woodland Park, Colorado. Surrounded by ample forest and given a lot of free rein, I quickly established an affinity for the natural world. That relationship, plus the relative isolation of my upbringing has, over time, become the primary influencers of my work.

With a palette of organic textures, I draw compositions comprised of familiar elements, yet present them in unsettling, unfamiliar contexts that reflect on larger themes, such as mortality or our sense of place and identity.
