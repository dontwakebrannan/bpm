Brannan McGill

Many visual artists represent their subject through paint strokes or pencil lines that individually don't carry much meaning, yet work together to create a composition that represents their subject. With a palette that reflects my long-held adoration for familiar organic materials and shapes, I explore this relationship between the final representation and the individual elements or textures that comprise it, occasionally to unsettling effect.
